How to make Colombian Arepas con Queso

Easy Recipe to Follow!

    Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Egg
  • Flour

    Instructions

  1. Add cornmeal, salt, and shredded queso fresco in a medium/large bowl. Whisk together to combine. Add water, milk, and butter to a sauce pan and heat until scalded (steaming but not boiling). Turn off heat. Add hot milk/water mixture to cornmeal mix. Stir with a wooden spoon until combined and then let sit for 5 minutes.
  2. Preheat griddle to medium-low (about 350 degrees). Grab a handful of dough (about â…“ cup, rounded) and form into a 5-inch patty. Add a pinch of shredded mozzarella into the center and fold the patty edges over the center to envelope the cheese into the dough, forming into a ball. Repeat with the rest of the dough. You should have 10 balls.
  3. Flatten each ball into a 4-5 inch patty, smoothing the edges if they start to crack. Place onto preheated griddle. Repeat with rest of the dough balls. Cook until both sides get dark spots from cooking and get firm on the outside. This should take about 4 minutes per side.
  4. Let cool for about 3 minutes and then serve. Serve hot.
  5. After letting it cool down, eat and enjoy!
